About Srinivas Restaurant

Srinivas Restaurant is a haven for delicious Indian cuisine, established in 2002. We are passionate about bringing the rich flavors and aromas of India to your table. Our mission is to provide a warm and inviting atmosphere where guests can connect over a satisfying and authentic Indian dining experience

We are committed to providing exceptional service to our guests. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff is dedicated to ensuring you have a memorable dining experience. We are happy to answer your questions and help you navigate our menu.

Our Menu

1.Malai Kofta (Main Course): Delicate vegetable dumplings in a creamy cashew and tomato gravy, a delightful vegetarian option.

2.Tandoori Chicken (Main Course): Marinated chicken pieces cooked in a clay tandoor oven, infused with smoky flavor and a popular choice.

3.Fish Curry (Main Course): Fresh fish cooked in a flavorful coconut-based curry, a seafood specialty.

4.Keema Naan (Bread): Flatbread stuffed with a spiced minced lamb filling, a savory and satisfying accompaniment.

5.Mango Ice Cream (Dessert): Creamy and refreshing ice cream made with real mangoes, a perfect way to end your meal on a sweet note.
