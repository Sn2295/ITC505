How to Play

Objective: The goal of "Lights Out" is to turn off all the lights on the board.

Instructions:

  • Click on any square to toggle its state and the state of adjacent squares.
  • A light that is on will turn off, and a light that is off will turn on.
  • The game starts with a random configuration of lights.

Tips:

  • Try to visualize the effect of each click on the entire board.
  • Use a systematic approach to avoid random clicking.
  • Consider solving one row or column at a time.
